ABOUT US


OUR MISSION

We are a unique, out-of-the-box health club, where our clients experience the phenomenal variety of fitness programs. Our mission is to eliminate monotony in workouts and make it a fun-filled, productive experience to bring about transformational changes in the lives of our clients. It is our passion to deliver a spectacular platform and motivate our local communities to engage in fitness, sports and work towards a healthy life, both physically and mentally.



OUR VISION

We aim to be a benchmark in the fitness industry, and to be a unique health club that practices a holistic approach on all aspects of fitness and well-being. Our vision is to be a premium world-class fitness club with an exceptional variation of programs to keep our clients interested and engaged in fitness. We aim to be the most trusted and respected health club, empowering our clients to reach their best in terms of physical, emotional and mental health.



OUR VALUES

No more repetitive workouts that will bore your muscles and mind! Every health-conscious individual understands the importance of being consistent in order to achieve their fitness goals. At Lotus Fitness we motivate, excite and assist our clients with a variation of exercise programs dispelling any monotony! We provide a spacious, well-ventilated platform to our clients where they can indulge in their choice of exercise, be it swimming, gym, Functional Training, HIIT, Yoga, Zumba or a game of football amongst various other interesting programs to reach their fitness goals. We are the only locally owned and operated health club of our size in the city. A single stop for all your athletic needs! Our highly experienced general trainers and personal trainers will motivate and assist you every step of the way in your fitness journey!
